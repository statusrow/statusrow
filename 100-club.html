We're working hard to raise funds in support of the Marine Conservation Society, but we're faced with costs of over £80,000 just to get to the start line. For our mission to be realised, we need to fund a boat, technical and life-saving equipment, on-shore support, food and other operational costs. There's a big list!

It's a huge target that we can only achieve through a combination of the team's savings, corporate sponsorship (our support-oars) and the 100 club.
